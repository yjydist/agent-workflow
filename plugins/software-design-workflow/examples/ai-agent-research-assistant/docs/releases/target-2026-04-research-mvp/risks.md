# Risks

## Citation drift

Summaries may lose the source mapping for factual claims. Mitigation: validate that every final brief section points back to captured note sources.

## Scope creep

Requests for automation or shared collaboration could expand the MVP. Mitigation: stop and ask for design clarification when work goes beyond the target baseline.

## Tool overreach

Broad research tools may invite unsupported source access. Mitigation: keep a narrow allowlist and refuse unclear source requests.
